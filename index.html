Most Recent Ongoing Project

Dholera

Located in Gujarat, Dholera Special Investment Region (SIR) is India's first planned smart city and a flagship initiative under the Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC). Spread across more than 920 sq. km, Dholera is envisioned as a world-class investment and infrastructure hub featuring advanced urban planning, sustainable development, and excellent connectivity.
